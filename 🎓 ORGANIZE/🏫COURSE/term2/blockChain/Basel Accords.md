#finance
* In order to ensure financial stability and reduce banking risks.These accords set rules on how much **capital and liquidity** banks must hold to absorb losses and avoid financial crises.
## Basel I
* Capital Adequacy Rules
* Introduced the **Capital Adequacy Ratio(CAR)**, requiring banks to hold capital equal to at least 8% of their risk-weighted assets.
* Classified assets into risk categories
* Purpose
	* Prevent banks from lending excessively without enough capital to cover losses.
