# Historical Perspective:Money & Ledgers
## Earlier Ledgers
* 7th BC: Standardized coinage (Lydians/Greeks)
* 14th :First multinational merchant banks
* 17th: Modern banking emerges
	* Deposit/lending system
	* Central banks established
	* Link to taxation
* 18th:Gold standard implement 金本位
	* Controlled money supply
	* Reduced financial risk
* 20th: [[Basel Accords]]巴塞尔协议 replace gold standard
	* Liquid assets become equivalent to gold reserves
	* Modern financial regulation begins
