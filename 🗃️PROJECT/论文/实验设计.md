### Experimental Setup

### LLM VS LLM

#### **Experimental Variables** (Each LLM undergoes 20 trials)

- **Number of Taboo Words**
  - 0 / 5
- **Target Word Type**
  - Concrete / Abstract
  - ~~Part of Speech (POS)~~ (excluded)
- **Game Rounds**
  - 5 / Unlimited
- **LLM Settings**
  - Temperature: 0 / 0.5

#### **Evaluation Metrics**

* For guesser

  - **Success Rate**
    - Measured using BERT/SBERT cosine similarity:
      - Exact match = **1.0**
      - Incorrect guess = **cosine similarity score**

  - **Number of Guesses Required**

* For hint giver

  * **Semantic Similarity Change**
    - Measured using **BERT/SBERT** to calculate the similarity between consecutive hints
  * **Taboo Word Violation Rate**
    - Percentage of hints that include restricted words

### **LLM Providing Hints vs. Human Guessing**



Test1 

* init prompt：
  * LLM guesser
    * We are playing the Taboo game. Your goal is to guess the **Target Word** without knowing it upfront. In each round, you will receive a **hint** from me, but the hint will not contain certain **Taboo Words** that are closely related to the Target Word. You need to guess the Target Word in as few attempts as possible. This round consists of **5 turns**. If you fail to guess the word within 5 turns, you lose the game.Think carefully and respond with your best guess in each round. Let's begin!
  * LLM hint provider
    * We are playing the Taboo game. Your role is to help the **Guesser** figure out the **Target Word** by providing effective **hints**. However, there are **Taboo Words** that you **cannot use** in your hints. These words are closely related to the Target Word and would make the game too easy. Your goal is to craft **clear, helpful, and creative hints** that guide the Guesser toward the correct answer without directly revealing it.  You can only use 1 sentence. Let’s begin!
